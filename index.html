Treaty of Waitangi
Main:
The Treaty of Waitangi is a document in New Zealand's history, signed on February 6 1840, between representatives of the British Crown and various Māori chiefs. It represents a agreement between both parties which facilitated the British colonization of New Zealand while promising protection to the Maori people
